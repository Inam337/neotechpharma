CEO's Message

Dear customers and patrons,It is an occasion of immense pride and pleasure to present the humble profile of the Neotech Pharmaceuticals, a distinctive industrial entity and honored pharmaceutical nucleus of the country to our valuable customers and patrons. I bow my head to the Almighty Allah, Who bestowed upon me His blessings in form of vision and wisdom, courage and resources to reshape my company proudly matching to the international standards. The Neotech is committed to produce quality veterinary medicines and nutritional products in the strong pursuit of the cherished objectives of quality, reliability and safety to guard and protect your precious and valuable treasure of livestock and poultry. I am honored and glad that the Neotech has achieved and introduced new dimensions and horizons in pharmaceutical industry of Pakistan. I offer my deep hearted gratitude to all our valuable customers and patrons who always reposed their confidence to us in making our company a trust worthy icon in the world of pharmacy. I am equally thankful to my colleagues and staff members, whose relentless endeavors, devotion and dedication made the present day success possible. Last but not least, my very special thanks to all our dignified veterinarians and farmers who always patronized us in moving forward by showing their trust in our products. May Almighty Allah bless us and give us more courage and strength to build better Pakistan and our industry with full dedication, devotion and commitment.

Dr. Naveed Iqbal MS-Biological Sciences Chief Executive Officer
